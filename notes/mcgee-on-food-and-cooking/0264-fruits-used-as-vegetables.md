# Fruits Used as Vegetables

Fruits Used as Vegetables

The botanical fruits that cooks treat as vegetables generally require cooking to make them interesting or soft enough to eat. The two familiar exceptions to this rule are tomatoes and cucumbers, frequently served raw in salads.
